The Cibeles

img: Alberto Nuñez


Cibeles is a very important monument of Madrid, here, you can find a historic place built a lot of years ago. When Real Madrid (football club) wins one important ligue or tournament, they go to celebrate the bictory on this monument.
If you go to Madrid, you need to visit Cibeles! The Cibeles Fountain (better known by the people of Madrid as La Cibeles) is a monumental fountain of the city of Madrid, located in the square of the same name, in the center of the Spanish capital.

It was conceived within an urban remodeling plan in the eighteenth century, on the initiative of King Carlos III, who planned to beautify the capital of the kingdom according to the aesthetics of neoclassicism.
The project included a series of fountains sculpted with classical mythological motifs (Fountain of Neptune.

Alfonso 12 Monument

This monument is a piece of art maked by Madrid antecesors. Alfonso 12 was a Spain's King.
When he died, a monumemt was contructed on his honor.

img: Robert Cruyf


In line with the memorials that were erected at the time and in other capitals of the world, the Cortes approved by Law of July 23, 1887 to erect an equestrian statue in Madrid to honor the memory of the ill-fated King Alfonso XII, personifying the Homeland, which was endorsed by his widow the Queen Regent Dª María Cristina de Habsburgo-Lorena.
